# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## 1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains our policies regarding refunds, cancellations, and billing for GeoScore subscriptions.

## 2. General Policy

### 2.1 No Refunds

All subscription fees are **non-refundable** except as:
- Required by applicable law
- Explicitly stated in this policy
- Approved at our sole discretion

### 2.2 Rationale

Our Service provides immediate access to:
- AI visibility tracking
- Brand analytics
- LLM sampling and analysis
- Historical data and reports

Once you access these features, we've delivered value and cannot "take back" the service provided.

## 3. Free Trials

### 3.1 Trial Period

- Free trials may be offered for new users
- Trial length varies (typically 7-14 days)
- Full access to plan features during trial
- No charges during trial period

### 3.2 Trial Cancellation

- Cancel anytime during trial period
- No charges if cancelled before trial ends
- Cancellation takes effect immediately
- Data is retained for 30 days

### 3.3 Trial to Paid Conversion

- Credit card may be required to start trial
- Automatic conversion to paid subscription after trial
- Email reminder sent before trial ends
- Cancel before trial ends to avoid charges

## 4. Subscription Cancellation

### 4.1 How to Cancel

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings â†’ Billing
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

Or contact support: support@geoscore.com

### 4.2 When Cancellation Takes Effect

- Cancellation takes effect at the end of current billing period
- You retain access until the end of the paid period
- No refund for unused time in current period
- No charges for future periods

### 4.3 After Cancellation

- Access to Service ends at period end

## 5. Downgrades

### 5.1 Plan Downgrades

- You can downgrade to a lower-tier plan anytime
- Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle
- No refund for current period
- Features may be limited immediately

### 5.2 Feature Limits

When downgrading:
- Existing data is retained
- Access may be limited to new plan limits
- Some features may become unavailable
- Historical data remains accessible

## 6. Upgrades

### 6.1 Plan Upgrades

- You can upgrade to a higher-tier plan anytime
- Upgrade takes effect immediately
- Prorated charge for remainder of billing period
- Next billing at new plan rate

### 6.2 Billing

- Immediate charge for prorated amount
- Example: Upgrade mid-month, pay 50% of price difference
- Next full charge at next billing cycle

## 7. Exceptional Circumstances

### 7.1 Service Issues

We may offer refunds if:
- Service is unavailable for extended period (>24 hours)
- Critical features are broken and not fixed promptly
- We fail to deliver promised service

**Process**:
1. Contact support immediately
2. Describe the issue
3. Allow us time to resolve (typically 48 hours)
4. Request refund if unresolved

### 7.2 Billing Errors

We will refund if:
- You were charged incorrectly
- Duplicate charges occurred
- Charges after cancellation

**Process**:
1. Contact support within 30 days
2. Provide transaction details
3. We'll investigate and refund if appropriate

### 7.3 Unauthorized Charges

If your account was compromised:
1. Contact support immediately
2. Change your password
3. Enable two-factor authentication
4. We'll investigate and may issue refund

### 8.1 How to Request

### 8.3 Refund Timeline
