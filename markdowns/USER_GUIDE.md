# GeoScore User Guide

## Welcome to GeoScore

GeoScore helps you track and optimize how your brand appears in AI-powered search results across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit the GeoScore application
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Choose your sign-up method:
   - Email and password
   - Phone number (SMS verification)
   - Google account
4. Complete verification
5. Set up 2FA (recommended)

### 2. Onboard Your First Brand

After signing up, you'll be guided through brand onboarding:

1. **Brand Information**
   - Enter your brand name
   - Add brand description
   - Specify your industry
   - Add website URL

2. **Add Competitors** (Optional)
   - Add 3-5 main competitors
   - This helps benchmark your AI visibility

3. **Define Topics**
   - Add topics relevant to your brand
   - These are used to test AI responses
   - Examples: "project management software", "team collaboration"

4. **Set Up Prompts**
   - Create prompts to test AI responses
   - Example: "What are the best project management tools?"
   - The system will track if your brand appears in responses

## Dashboard Overview

### Main Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **AI Visibility Score**: Overall brand visibility across LLMs
- **Mention Trends**: How often your brand is mentioned over time
- **Competitor Comparison**: Your visibility vs. competitors
- **Recent Jobs**: Status of AI sampling jobs
- **Quick Actions**: Start new analysis, add prompts, etc.

### Key Metrics

1. **Visibility Score (0-100)**
   - How often your brand appears in AI responses
   - Higher is better

2. **Mention Rate**
   - Percentage of prompts where your brand is mentioned
   - Tracked per LLM provider

3. **Ranking Position**
   - Where your brand appears in AI responses
   - Position 1-3 is ideal

4. **Source Citations**
   - Which sources LLMs cite about your brand
   - Helps identify content opportunities

## Core Features

### AI Visibility Tracking

**Purpose**: Monitor how LLMs respond to prompts about your industry

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "AI Visibility" page
2. View visibility scores by provider (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity)
3. See mention trends over time
4. Analyze which prompts perform best

### Competitor Analysis

**Purpose**: Compare your AI visibility against competitors

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "Competitors" page
2. View competitor visibility scores
3. See head-to-head comparisons
4. Identify gaps and opportunities

### Prompt Management

**Purpose**: Create and manage test prompts

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "Prompts" page
2. Click "Add Prompt"
3. Enter your prompt text
4. Select target LLM providers
5. Click "Sample Now" to test
6. View results and analysis

**Best Practices**:
- Create prompts that match user search intent
- Test different prompt variations
- Include industry-specific terms
- Monitor prompt performance over time

### Source Intelligence

**Purpose**: Track which sources LLMs cite about your brand

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "Sources" page
2. View all sources citing your brand
3. See citation frequency
4. Identify high-value content sources

**Actions**:
- Prioritize content on frequently-cited domains
- Build relationships with key publishers
- Create content for underrepresented topics

### Brand Context Engine

**Purpose**: Provide LLMs with accurate brand information

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "Brand Profile"
2. Click "Brand Context"
3. Add key information:
   - Company overview
   - Key features
   - Use cases
   - Target audience
   - Differentiators

**Why It Matters**:
- Helps LLMs understand your brand
- Improves accuracy of AI responses
- Increases mention quality

### Content Optimization

**Purpose**: Get AI-powered recommendations to improve visibility

**How to Use**:
1. Go to "Content" page
2. View optimization suggestions
3. See recommended topics
4. Get content ideas based on gaps

## Advanced Features

### AXP (AI Experience Pages)

Create optimized pages for AI consumption:

1. Go to "Content" → "AXP Pages"
2. Click "Create Page"
3. Add structured content
4. Include schema markup
5. Publish and monitor performance

### FAQ Management

Manage frequently asked questions:

1. Go to "Content" → "FAQs"
2. Add common questions about your brand
3. Provide clear, concise answers
4. LLMs may use these in responses

### Schema Markup

Add structured data for better AI understanding:

1. Go to "Content" → "Schema"
2. Add organization schema
3. Include product/service schemas
4. Validate and publish

## Troubleshooting

### Jobs Not Running

- Check your subscription limits
- Verify API keys are configured
- Contact support if issues persist

### Low Visibility Scores

- Review your brand context
- Create more targeted content
- Increase content on cited sources
- Test different prompt variations

### Missing Data

- Ensure jobs have completed
- Check date range filters
- Verify brand setup is complete
